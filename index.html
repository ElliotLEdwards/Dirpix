Who's Diprix ?

Dirpix is a 23-year-old artist from Lyon. Multi-instrumentalist from a young age, he discovered Ableton Live in 2016, a music creation software, and began producing his own music the following year. In 2020, the young DJ was spotted and invited by the Lyon giant Pyramiid Production, to play in places like Le Petit Salon or the Ninkasi Gerland alongside internationally renowned artists with the organisation Pyramiid Production
